Master Chief — Details

Master Chief is the main protagonist of the Halo series. He is a genetically enhanced supersoldier known as a Spartan-II. Wearing advanced Mjolnir armor, he leads humanity’s fight against alien threats.

  • First Appearance: Halo (2001)
  • Abilities: Enhanced strength, reflexes, tactics
  • Role: Military supersoldier
  • Personality: Calm, tactical, mission-focused
